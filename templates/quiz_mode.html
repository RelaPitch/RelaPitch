What is a Reference Note?

The reference note is the starting point for relative pitch - it's where the "relative" in relative pitch comes from. It serves as a foundation that helps you identify or sing other notes during the quiz.

  • Choose a note that's comfortable for you to hear or sing
  • You will first play this note in the beginning for each quiz question
  • You'll then identify or sing other notes in relation to this reference note
